## Key Insights for Market Regime Prediction

### Volatility Spikes (Market Impact):
1. **Volcker Era (1979-1982)**: Highest rate volatility ever; mortgage rates 18-20%; unemployment 7%+; worst bear market since WWII
2. **2022-2023 Hiking Cycle**: Fastest tightening in 40 years; tech stocks down 70%; bond yields inverted
3. **Emergency Cuts (2001, 2008, 2020)**: Preceded by market shocks; equities stabilized within 6 months

### Turning Points (Regime Shifts):
- **1975 (trough)**: Recession bottom; recovery starts (Bull Market of 1975)
- **1982 (trough)**: Inflation broken; Bull Market of 1982 begins (historic 20-year rally)
- **2002 (trough)**: Tech recovery; housing boom starts
- **2009 (trough)**: QE begins; 11-year bull market starts
- **2022 (peak)**: Rates peak; easing phase begins 2024+

### Predictive Signal:
- **Rate hikes >50bps monthly** = Tightening cycle (next 3-6 months volatility expected)
- **Rate cuts >50bps monthly** = Easing cycle OR crisis response (equities often recover within 6 months)
- **Rate pauses (0±0.1%)** = Neutral stance; look to forward guidance for direction
